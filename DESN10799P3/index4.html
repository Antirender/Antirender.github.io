Snapchat Data Transparency

Create Account

Please fill out your basic details to get started. By tapping “Agree and Continue” below, you agree to the Terms of Service and acknowledge that you have read the Privacy Policy.

Find All Your Friends

Allow Full Access for Snapchat to use your contacts to give you the best friend suggestions, or choose Select Contacts for individual contacts. We upload your contacts to our servers only when you grant permission.

If you prefer not to share your full contact list, you can manually add friends. Learn more about contact usage.

Photo Library Access

Grant Snapchat permission to access your camera roll so you can share photos or videos with friends, and personalize your experience. You can choose Limit Access (e.g., only selected images), Allow Full Access (entire photo library), or Don’t Allow.

Photos often contain metadata like location, depth information, captions, or audio in videos. We only use this data to provide relevant features and do not share it with third-party advertisers without your explicit consent. Learn more.

Microphone Access

Snapchat uses your microphone to record audio for Snaps, video chats, and some Lenses that respond to voice input. You can opt out at any time in your device’s app settings.

Location Controls

Ghost Mode hides your live location from everyone. You can also share your location with Friends Only, or choose specific friends under Only These Friends... for complete privacy control. The default location sharing can be changed anytime in Settings.

Ghost Mode
When enabled, nobody can see your live location. You’ll still get core app features, but location-based filters may be limited.
My Friends Only
Shares location with friends you’ve added. This helps them know if you’re nearby.

Data Dashboard

Snaps & Storage: Snaps are deleted once viewed, but unopened Snaps remain on our servers for up to 30 days so they can be delivered if opened late. Some metadata may persist for troubleshooting or legal compliance. Learn more.

Save Snaps to Memories
Save your Snaps in Memories on our servers, accessible only by you (unless you share them). If turned off, snaps won’t remain after viewing.
Personalized Ads & Filters
Helps us show relevant Lenses or ads based on your interactions and location. You can disable this to reduce data usage, but some features may be less personalized.
Note: Changing these settings may affect your experience. You can return to the Settings at any time to update preferences. For complete details, see our Privacy Policy.

Step 1: Creating Your Account

Enter your details and tap “Agree and Continue” to accept the real Snapchat Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Step 2: Contacts Access

Choose Allow Full Access or Select Contacts to add your friends. Snapchat uploads this data only if you allow it, to help suggest potential connections.

Step 3: Photo Library

Decide how you share your photos. Limit Access or Allow Full Access if you want your entire camera roll available for snaps or stories.

Step 4: Microphone Usage

Let Snapchat record audio for snaps, video chat, and certain Lenses. You can disable microphone permission at any time in your device settings.

Step 5: Location & Ghost Mode

Manage who can see your live location. Enable Ghost Mode to stay hidden, or share with friends or custom lists.

Step 6: Data Dashboard

Review ephemeral snap rules, toggle Memories on or off, and control personalization under the Privacy Controls in Settings.
