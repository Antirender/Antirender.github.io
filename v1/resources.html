Gardening Tips

  • Watering Wisdom: Water your plants early in the morning to reduce evaporation and ensure roots absorb enough moisture.
  • Soil Health: Regularly add compost to your soil to boost nutrients and improve plant growth.
  • Pruning Techniques: Prune dead or dying branches to promote healthy growth and flowering.

Recommended Plants

  • Lavender: Drought-tolerant and perfect for sunny areas, lavender is great for pollinators like bees and butterflies.
  • Tomatoes: A classic for any garden, tomatoes grow well in sunny spots with plenty of water and rich soil.
  • Hostas: Ideal for shady gardens, hostas offer lush, beautiful foliage and are easy to maintain.

Eco-Friendly Gardening Techniques

  • Composting: Reduce waste by composting kitchen scraps and garden clippings, turning them into nutrient-rich soil.
  • Mulching: Use organic mulch to retain moisture in the soil, reduce weeds, and improve soil quality.
  • Rainwater Harvesting: Collect rainwater in barrels to water your plants and reduce water consumption.
